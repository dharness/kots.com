Bablot

Bablot is a communications tool for Property Rental managers. It uses Facebook messenger to automate responses, and turn after hour visitors into valuable leads. After designing the web portal, we implemented the app in Angular 2 and Node, then finally deployed with Docker. Once the product was ready we moved to branding, logo design, proposal writing, marketing, and sales.

To top it all off we crafted 7 custom AI chat bots from the ground up for a variety of property rental managers.
