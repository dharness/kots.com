Our Portfolio

Sqwak

Sqwak allows you to train and test your own custom audio classifier without writing any code! All you have to do is upload some sound files, and we use Tensorflow to extract features and train a classifier.

We then make your classifier available at an API endpoint so that developers can classify sounds on the fly, straight from their games or apps.

Bablot

Bablot is a communications tool for Property Rental managers. It uses Facebook messenger to automate responses, and turn after hour visitors into valuable leads. After designing the web portal, we implemented the app in Angular 2 and Node, then finally deployed with Docker.

To top it all off we crafted 7 custom AI chat bots from the ground up for a variety of property rental managers.

Beamer

Beamer is an unopinionated CMS for bloggers and creators. It provides a rich interface for creating, storing, and managing content but gives you complete freedom over how you use that content.

Beamer provides a full RESTful api for developers to inject their content wherever and whenever they want.

Botany

Botany is changing the way people make chat bots. We designed custom software to let people create intelligent chatbots with no code. Botany features tons of plugins that enhance bots with things like payments, booking appointments, and more!

For developers, we built an open API to give anyone the power to integrate chat bots into their existing platform.

Valia

Valia is your go-to platform for finding and listing rental properties in Peru. Whether you're searching for your dream home or listing your property for rent, Valia's user-friendly interface powered by React and Node.js makes the process a breeze.

Designed by our expert team, Valia offers a seamless experience for both renters and property owners, ensuring a smooth and efficient rental process. With Valia, you can explore a wide range of properties, filter based on your preferences, and connect directly with property owners. It's a one-stop solution for all your rental property needs in Peru!
